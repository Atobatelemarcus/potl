DATA ANALYTICS

MANUFACTURING DOWNTIME PROJECT

This project involves analysing the raw data of a soda bottling plant which experienced several downtime over the month due to two major reasons ;The operational and machine downtime. The operational downtime is caused by lack of technical knowledge or mistake of the operator. while the machine downtime is caused by sudden, unpredictable misbehaviour of the machines. The analysis highlighted notable keyfindings and insights from this raw data

SUPERSTORE SALES

This project involves analysing the data of of a mega store.The aim of the project is to unveil key performance indicator (KPI) which influence the sales of products across the country.The analysis revealed the finacial state of the store the chart reveals the KPI ,insight and recommendations were given

SUPPLY CHAIN

The data analysis is of a supply chain of a production company with several sections namely; Production, stock & inventory and logistics . The analysis was centered on unveilling the position of the company through several key indicators of the data; Net cost of production, revenue and the total profit acroos every sections .Although each section has their peculiar driven key indicator to look out for.
